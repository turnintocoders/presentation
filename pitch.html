Turn into Coders

A full-stack web developer bootcamp

in JavaScript

Who am I?

Matteo Giaccone, computer engineer.

Founder and CEO of a software consultancy cooperative for 3 years.

Full-stack employee/consultant for 4+ years.

Founded Turn into Coders to bring modernity to Italy.

OK, what do you teach?

We'll work together in a highly collaborative manner through frontend development, Node.JS, PostgreSQL.

You'll be able to build fully interactive responsive web applications, with a database, users and all the bells and whistles. And they will be scalable too.

How long it will take?

  • 4 weeks: pre-bootcamp, to work on prerequisites
  • 8 weeks: the bootcamp per-se
  • 4 weeks: recommended time to stay around with your team to work on your first serious project
  • 8 weeks: you can still use the campus to meet companies or start your projects. Mentors will be around for help

Is that realistic?

We believe that real understanding should take its time.

We don't squeeze knowledge into the schedule, but we teach you how to learn.
