Turn into Coders

Is a serious full-stack web developer bootcamp

in JavaScript

based in Turin - Italy

What do you mean by serious?

We believe that real learning should take a decent amount of time, we don't promise the moon.

OK, what do you teach?

We'll work together in a highly collaborative manner through frontend development (with a splash of React), Node.JS, PostgreSQL.

You'll be able to build fully interactive responsive web applications, with a database, users and all the whistles. And they will be able to scale.

How long it will take?

  • 4 weeks: pre-bootcamp, to work on prerequisites
  • 8 weeks: the bootcamp per-se
  • 4 weeks: recommended time to stay around with your team to work on your first serious project
  • 8 weeks: you can still use the campus to meet companies or start your projects. Mentors will be around for help

Don't worry you will still get time to enjoy your stay in Italy.

Where?

We have a very nice room in Toolbox coworking, the best coworking space in Turin (I think!).

Our neighbours are the local FabLab plus a good number of tech companies. Every week there are many events, techy and not so much.
