Turn into Coders

Is a serious full-stack web developer bootcamp

in JavaScript

based in Turin - Italy

What do you mean by serious?

We believe that real learning should take a decent amount of time, we don't promise the moon

OK, what do you teach?

We'll work together in a highly collaborative manner through frontend development (with a splash of React), Node.JS, PostgreSQL

You'll be able to build fully interactive responsive web applications, with users, a database, in a scalable fashion

How long it will take?

The bootcamp per-se is 8 weeks long, but we offer a 4 weeks pre-bootcamp to work on prerequisites and we recommend you to stay for 4 more weeks afterwards to build your first serious project, with your team

Then you'll be able to stay for 8 more weeks to start freelancing, job hunting or building your startup in a safe environment, with mentors to help when you get stuck

Don't worry you will still get time to enjoy your stay in Italy.

Where?

We have a very nice room in Toolbox coworking, the best coworking space in Turin (I think!).

Our neighbours are the local FabLab plus a good number of tech companies. Every week there are many events, techy and not so much

Who are you?

The founder, Matteo Giaccone, is a computer engineer. He founded WeLaika 7 years ago. He now works for a British Company. He is investing in Turn into Coders to bring modernity to Italy. He knows everyone in Turin working with IT.
